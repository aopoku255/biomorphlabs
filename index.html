Packaging began with nature

leaves, gourds, and woven baskets

Then came plastic

convenient, but polluting and permanent.

Now, Biomorph Labs uses alternative fibre sources to produce paper for packaging compostable, circular, and climate-smart.

What We’re Building

We’re creating packaging materials from agricultural waste like banana and plantain stems. Our tree-free, compostable packaging is designed for a world that’s ready to move beyond plastic and deforestation.
